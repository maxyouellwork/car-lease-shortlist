💡 How this differs from leasing

On HP you pay monthly then own the car outright — so at the end you've got an asset worth something (a few grand resale), not nothing. £0 deposit here.

The honest trade-off: these are used & older, and once any warranty runs out you cover MOT, servicing & repairs yourself (a lease includes all that). So on used, reliability matters more than gadgets — budget ~£40–60/mo for upkeep on top.

⚠️ Why no Peugeot 2008 / 308 here

There are tempting low-mileage Peugeot 2008s at this price — but the 1.2 PureTech petrol engine has a "wet belt" that can fail expensively. One listing literally said "wet belt replaced." On a car you'll own, I've left them off unless you find one with proof the belt's been done. (Same for Citroën C4 / 308 PureTech.)
