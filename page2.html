💡 Own it = HP, not PCP

These all let you pay monthly then own the car (Hire Purchase). Monthlies are estimates over 4 years at £0 deposit — exact figure depends on the APR you're offered, so use the "live deals" links to confirm. A 5-year term knocks ~£40–50/mo off if you want it lower.

⚠️ Beware "£300/mo" ads on £15k+ cars — those are usually PCP (big balloon at the end; you don't own it). To truly own on HP, ~£250–320/mo over 4 yrs = a £10–12k car.
