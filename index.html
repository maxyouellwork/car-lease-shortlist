🆚 Tiggo 4 vs Tiggo 7 — the 4 is the pricey one

The Tiggo 4 leases for £306/mo at just 5,000 miles with a £2,760 deposit (poor value, 48/100). The bigger Tiggo 7 is ~£290/mo at 10k miles, 1-month deposit, scores better & gets the panoramic roof. Go for the 7, not the 4.

📷 Cameras, sensors & the panoramic roof

At these prices you get reversing camera + parking sensors, wireless CarPlay, adaptive cruise. A full 360° surround camera needs the next trim up (~£310–330). 🌅 For a panoramic sunroof, the Chery Tiggo 7 Summit is the pick (≈ base price). The Peugeot 2008 doesn't offer one.
