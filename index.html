🆚 Tiggo 4 vs Tiggo 7 — the 4 is actually the expensive one

The Tiggo 4 leases for £306/mo even at 5,000 miles with a big 9-month deposit (≈£2,760 upfront) — and LeaseLoco rates it poor value (48/100). The bigger Tiggo 7 is ~£290/mo at 10k miles with a 1-month deposit, scores better, and gets the panoramic roof. So the Tiggo 7 wins — don't pay more for the smaller 4.

📷 Cameras, sensors & the panoramic roof — what the money buys

At £226–297/mo you reliably get reversing camera + parking sensors, wireless CarPlay/Android Auto, adaptive cruise, big screens. A true 360° surround camera only appears on the next trim up (~£310–330/mo).

🌅 Panoramic sunroof: the Chery Tiggo 7 Summit has a proper electric tilt/slide pano roof — for about the same price as the base trim. (The Peugeot 2008 doesn't offer one.)
